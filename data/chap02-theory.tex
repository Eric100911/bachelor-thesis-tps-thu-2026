\section{质子结构与部分子关联}
% \section{Proton Structure and Parton Correlations}

% TO_APPROVE

% [PENDING]
在标准共线因子化框架下，质子由单部分子分布函数（PDF）$f_i(x,Q^2)$描述，它给出在因子化标度$Q^2$下探测质子时，找到携带纵向动量分数$x$、味为$i$的部分子的概率密度。PDF的$Q^2$依赖性由DGLAP演化方程决定，并已在深度非弹性散射和强子对撞机的硬散射过程中得到了高精度检验。

% In the standard collinear factorisation framework, the proton is described by single-parton distribution functions (PDFs) $f_i(x, Q^2)$, which give the probability density of finding a parton of flavor $i$ carrying a longitudinal momentum fraction $x$ when the proton is probed at a factorisation scale $Q^2$. The $Q^2$-dependence of the PDFs is governed by the DGLAP evolution equations and has been tested to high precision in deep-inelastic scattering and in hard-scattering processes at hadron colliders.

% TO_APPROVE

% [PENDING]
然而，共线PDF是单体对象：它们既不包含质子内部部分子横向空间分布的信息，也不包含多个部分子之间关联的信息。在LHC上，质子在低纵向动量分数$x$和高部分子密度的区域被探测，在此区域中胶子数量迅速增长。在这种环境下，同一次质子--质子碰撞中不止一对部分子发生硬散射的概率变得不可忽略。因此，质子的横向部分子轮廓及其组分部分子之间的关联——包括味、动量、自旋和色的关联——通过对多重硬散射敏感的观测量变得可实验探测~\cite{Z48W3VLV,Diehl:2018:DPS-theory}。

% Collinear PDFs, however, are one-body objects: they carry no information about the transverse spatial distribution of partons inside the proton, nor about correlations among multiple partons. At the LHC, the proton is probed in the regime of low longitudinal momentum fraction $x$ and high parton density, where the number of gluons grows rapidly. In this environment, the probability that more than one parton pair from the same proton--proton collision undergoes a hard scattering becomes non-negligible. The proton's transverse parton profile and the correlations among its constituent partons --- in flavor, momentum, spin, and color --- thus become experimentally accessible through observables that are sensitive to multiple hard scatters~\cite{Z48W3VLV,Diehl:2018:DPS-theory}.

\section{从单部分子散射到多重部分子散射}
% \section{From Single- to Multiple-Parton Scattering}

% TO_APPROVE

% [PENDING]
在通常的硬质子--质子碰撞主导图像中，每个入射质子各有一个部分子参与短程部分子子过程，其余组分形成束流剩余和底层事例。这种机制称为单部分子散射（SPS），是对高标度末态进行微扰计算的默认框架。由部分子$i,j$产生末态$A,B$的SPS截面为

\begin{equation}
  \sigma_\mathrm{SPS}^{AB} = \sum_{i,j} \int dx_1 dx_2 \, f_i(x_1, Q^2) \, f_j(x_2, Q^2) \, \hat{\sigma}_{ij \to AB}(x_1, x_2, Q^2) .
\end{equation}

% In the usual leading picture of a hard proton--proton collision, one parton from each incoming proton participates in a short-distance partonic subprocess, while the remaining constituents form the beam remnants and the underlying event. This mechanism is called single-parton scattering (SPS) and is the default framework for perturbative calculations of high-scale final states. The SPS cross section for producing final states $A,B$ from partons $i,j$ is

在LHC上，低$x$胶子丰富区域中巨大的部分子亮度使得两对或更多对部分子能在同一次质子--质子碰撞中发生独立的硬散射~\cite{Z48W3VLV,denterriaDoubleTripleNparton2017}。这类事例统称为多重部分子散射（MPS），其中最常讨论的是双部分子散射（DPS）和三重部分子散射（TPS）。MPS探测了单部分子测量无法触及的质子结构维度。SPS截面约束单体PDF $f_i(x,Q^2)$，而MPS观测量则对两个或更多部分子的联合分布敏感，包括它们的相对横向位置~\cite{Diehl:2018:DPS-theory}。由于这些关联是非微扰输入，必须从多种末态和不同散射重数的测量中提取~\cite{Z48W3VLV,Blok:2017:MPI-correlations}。

% At the LHC, the large parton luminosity in the gluon-rich region of low $x$ makes it possible for two or more parton pairs to undergo separate hard scatterings in the same proton--proton collision~\cite{Z48W3VLV,denterriaDoubleTripleNparton2017}. Such events are collectively referred to as multiple-parton scattering (MPS), with double-parton scattering (DPS) and triple-parton scattering (TPS) as the most commonly discussed cases. MPS probes dimensions of proton structure that are inaccessible to single-parton measurements. Whereas SPS cross sections constrain the one-body PDFs $f_i(x,Q^2)$, MPS observables are sensitive to the joint distributions of two or more partons, including their relative transverse positions~\cite{Diehl:2018:DPS-theory}. Because these correlations are nonperturbative inputs, they must be extracted from measurements in multiple final states and at different scattering multiplicities~\cite{Z48W3VLV,Blok:2017:MPI-correlations}.

多重部分子散射的形式化QCD描述需要将因子化框架扩展到单PDF之外。双部分子分布函数（dPDF）$D_{ij}(x_1, x_2, \mathbf{b}; Q_1^2, Q_2^2)$描述了以相对横向距离$\mathbf{b}$提取味为$i$和$j$、纵向动量分数分别为$x_1$和$x_2$的两个部分子的联合概率~\cite{Gaunt:2010:dPDF,Diehl:2018:DPS-theory}。与单PDF不同，dPDF同时编码了质子的纵向和横向结构。其QCD演化也与DGLAP不同：单个部分子微扰$1\to 2$劈裂为两个随后都发生硬散射的部分子，这引入了单PDF演化中不存在的非齐次项，在小$\mathbf{b}$处产生强关联。

% The formal QCD description of multiple parton scattering requires extending the factorisation framework beyond single PDFs. Double parton distribution functions (dPDFs) $D_{ij}(x_1, x_2, \mathbf{b}; Q_1^2, Q_2^2)$ describe the joint probability of extracting two partons of flavors $i$ and $j$ with longitudinal momentum fractions $x_1$ and $x_2$ at a relative transverse separation $\mathbf{b}$~\cite{Gaunt:2010:dPDF,Diehl:2018:DPS-theory}. Unlike single PDFs, dPDFs encode both the longitudinal and the transverse structure of the proton. Their QCD evolution differs from DGLAP: the perturbative $1\to 2$ splitting of a single parton into two partons that both undergo hard scatterings introduces an inhomogeneous term absent from single-PDF evolution, which generates strong correlations at small $\mathbf{b}$.

进一步的简化——独立散射近似——提供了MPS最常用的唯象框架。各次独立硬散射之间的关联被忽略，复杂的dPDF结构由单一参数$\sigma_\mathrm{eff}$概括。对于$n$个硬末态$A_1,\ldots,A_n$，因子化的MPS截面为~\cite{denterriaDoubleTripleNparton2017}

\begin{equation}
  \label{eq:nps-pocket}
  \sigma_{\mathrm{NPS}}^{A_1+\cdots+A_n}
  =
  \frac{\mathfrak{m}}{n!}
  \frac{\prod_{i=1}^{n}\sigma_{\mathrm{SPS}}^{A_i}}
       {(\sigma_{\mathrm{eff},\,\mathrm{NPS}})^{\,n-1}} .
\end{equation}

% [PENDING]
其中$\mathfrak{m}$是组合因子，用于处理全同末态并去除重复分配。对于与本论文最相关的DPS（$n=2$）和TPS（$n=3$）情况，

\begin{equation}
  \label{eq:dps-pocket}
  \sigma_{\mathrm{DPS}}^{A+B}
  =
  \frac{\mathfrak{m}}{2}
  \frac{\sigma_{\mathrm{SPS}}^{A}\,\sigma_{\mathrm{SPS}}^{B}}
       {\sigma_{\mathrm{eff},\,\mathrm{DPS}}},
  \qquad
  \mathfrak{m}=
  \begin{cases}
    1, & A = B,\\
    2, & A \neq B,
  \end{cases}
\end{equation}

\begin{equation}
  \label{eq:tps-pocket}
  \sigma_{\mathrm{TPS}}^{A+B+C}
  =
  \frac{\mathfrak{m}}{3!}

  \mathfrak{m}=
  \begin{cases}
    1, & A = B = C,\\
    3, & \text{exactly two identical},\\
    6, & \text{all distinct}.
  \end{cases}
\end{equation}

\section{QCD 中的重夸克偶素产生}
% \section{Heavy Quarkonium Production in QCD}

高能质子--质子碰撞中的重夸克偶素产生是量子色动力学（QCD）的核心检验场所。重夸克质量$m_Q \gg \Lambda_\mathrm{QCD}$为短程重夸克--反夸克对的产生提供了微扰标度，而色中性束缚态的形成则涉及非微扰动力学。在非相对论QCD（NRQCD）因子化框架下，产生截面表示为微扰QCD可计算的短程系数乘以编码每个中间$\mathrm{Q}\bar{\mathrm{Q}}$组态强子化概率的普适长程矩阵元之和~\cite{NRQCD_BBL1995}。这种双重性使得夸克偶素测量对微扰计算和强子化模型都敏感，LHC各实验广泛的微分截面、极化和关联测量计划为NRQCD方法提供了严格的检验~\cite{LANSBERG_QUARKONIUM_REVIEW,zhaoExperimentalReviewQuarkonium2025}。

% Heavy-quarkonium production in high-energy proton--proton collisions is a central testing ground for quantum chromodynamics (QCD). The heavy-quark mass provides a perturbative scale for the short-distance production of a heavy quark--antiquark pair, while the formation of a color-neutral bound state involves nonperturbative dynamics. In the nonrelativistic QCD (NRQCD) factorisation framework, the production cross section is expressed as a sum of short-distance coefficients --- computable in perturbative QCD --- multiplied by universal long-distance matrix elements that encode the hadronisation probability for each intermediate $\mathrm{Q}\bar{\mathrm{Q}}$ configuration~\cite{NRQCD_BBL1995}. This dual nature makes quarkonium measurements sensitive to both perturbative calculations and hadronisation models, and an extensive programme of differential cross-section, polarisation, and correlation measurements across the LHC experiments has provided stringent tests of the NRQCD approach~\cite{LANSBERG_QUARKONIUM_REVIEW,zhaoExperimentalReviewQuarkonium2025}.

% The production of a heavy quarkonium state in high-energy hadron collisions is a multi-scale process that tests both perturbative and nonperturbative quantum chromodynamics (QCD). The subsequent evolution of the $Q\bar{Q}$ pair into a physical color-neutral bound state involves long-distance dynamics that must be treated nonperturbatively.

\begin{equation}
  \sigma(pp \to H+X) = \sum_n \mathrm{SDC}(Q\bar{Q}[^{2S+1}L_J^{[c]}]) \times \langle\mathcal{O}^H[n]\rangle .
\end{equation}

The long-distance matrix elements (LDMEs) $\langle\mathcal{O}^H[n]\rangle$ encode the nonperturbative probability for the intermediate $Q\bar{Q}$ configuration to evolve into the physical quarkonium state. The LDMEs are assumed to be universal — independent of the production process — and are extracted from global fits to experimental data. The expansion is organised in powers of $v$, the relative velocity of the heavy quarks in the bound state; for $J/\psi$, $v^2 \approx 0.3$, and for $\phi(1020)$, the strange-quark mass is too light for a nonrelativistic expansion to apply rigorously.

% Three broad classes of quarkonium production models coexist in the literature. The Color-Singlet Model (CSM) assumes that the $Q\bar{Q}$ pair is produced directly with the same quantum numbers as the physical quarkonium; it fails to describe high-transverse-momentum $J/\psi$ production at the Tevatron and the LHC by one to two orders of magnitude. The Color-Octet Mechanism (COM), embedded in NRQCD, allows the $Q\bar{Q}$ pair to be produced in an intermediate color-octet state that subsequently radiates soft gluons to form a color-singlet bound state; this is the most complete framework and has been extensively tested against LHC measurements of differential cross sections and polarisation observables~\cite{LANSBERG_QUARKONIUM_REVIEW,zhaoExperimentalReviewQuarkonium2025}.

The cross section for a given quarkonium state $H$ is then obtained by integrating the $Q\bar{Q}$ production cross section over the invariant-mass range from $2m_Q$ to the open-heavy-flavor threshold, multiplying by the statistical color-singlet probability $1/9$ from $\mathrm{SU}(3)$ counting, and applying a universal hadronisation fraction $\rho_H$ for each state~\cite{LANSBERG_QUARKONIUM_REVIEW}. While the CEM cannot predict polarisation and assumes process-independent production ratios that are contradicted by data, its simplicity and its connection to quark--hadron duality make it a useful phenomenological reference.

% The $J/\psi$ meson ($m = 3.097~\mathrm{GeV}$, $\Gamma \approx 93~\mathrm{keV}$) is reconstructed via its dimuon decay $J/\psi \to \mu^+\mu^-$, with a branching fraction $\mathcal{B} = (5.961 \pm 0.033)\%$~\cite{PDG2024}. % The $\phi(1020)$ meson ($m = 1.019~\mathrm{GeV}$, $\Gamma \approx 4.2~\mathrm{MeV}$) is a vector meson with valence quark content approximately $s\bar{s}$, reconstructed in the $K^+K^-$ decay channel with $\mathcal{B} = (49.2 \pm 0.5)\%$~\cite{PDG2024}. In this analysis, $\phi$ candidates are selected above a minimum transverse-momentum threshold, which selects hard-scattering $\phi$ production and suppresses the soft $\phi$ background from the underlying event. This allows the $\phi$ to serve as a hard probe of parton-level dynamics, on a similar footing to the $J/\psi$, despite the absence of a heavy-quark mass scale.
