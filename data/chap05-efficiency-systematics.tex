Following the general presentation used in public CMS quarkonium analyses, this chapter decomposes the correction factors into the acceptance, reconstruction efficiency, identification efficiency, trigger efficiency, and any necessary event-level correction factors.
Even if this analysis does not ultimately report a physical cross section, the role of these correction factors in the interpretation of the candidate yield should still be stated explicitly.
