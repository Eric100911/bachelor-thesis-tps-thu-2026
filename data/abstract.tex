% 中英文摘要和关键字

\begin{abstract}
  论文的摘要是对论文研究内容和成果的高度概括。
  % The thesis abstract is a concise summary of the research content and results.

% [PENDING]
  摘要应对论文所研究的问题及其研究目的进行描述，对研究方法和过程进行简单介绍，对研究成果和所得结论进行概括。
  % It should describe the research problem and objectives, briefly introduce the research methods and process, and summarize the research results and conclusions.

% [PENDING]
  摘要应具有独立性和自明性，其内容应包含与论文全文同等量的主要信息。
  % The abstract should be independent and self-contained, and should include the principal information contained in the full thesis.

% [PENDING]
  使读者即使不阅读全文，通过摘要就能了解论文的总体内容和主要成果。
  % It should allow readers to understand the overall content and main results of the thesis without reading the full text.

% [PENDING]
  论文摘要的书写应力求精确、简明。
  % The abstract should be written precisely and concisely.

% [PENDING]
  切忌写成对论文书写内容进行提要的形式，尤其要避免"第 1 章……；第 2 章……；……"这种或类似的陈述方式。
  % It should not be written as a chapter-by-chapter outline of the thesis; in particular, statements such as ``Chapter 1 ...; Chapter 2 ...; ...'' or similar forms should be avoided.

% [PENDING]
  关键词是为了文献标引工作、用以表示全文主要内容信息的单词或术语。
  % Keywords are words or terms used for literature indexing and for indicating the main content of the thesis.

% [PENDING]
  关键词不超过 5 个，每个关键词中间用分号分隔。
  % No more than five keywords should be provided, separated by semicolons.

  % 关键词用"英文逗号"分隔，输出时会自动处理为正确的分隔符
  \thusetup{
    % keywords = {关键词 1, 关键词 2, 关键词 3, 关键词 4, 关键词 5},
    keywords = {keyword 1, keyword 2, keyword 3, keyword 4, keyword 5},
  }
\end{abstract}

\begin{abstract*}
  An abstract of a dissertation is a summary and extraction of research work and contributions.
  Included in an abstract should be description of research topic and research objective, brief introduction to methodology and research process, and summary of conclusion and contributions of the research.
  An abstract should be characterized by independence and clarity and carry identical information with the dissertation.
  It should be such that the general idea and major contributions of the dissertation are conveyed without reading the dissertation.

% [PENDING]
  An abstract should be concise and to the point.
  It is a misunderstanding to make an abstract an outline of the dissertation and words "the first chapter", "the second chapter" and the like should be avoided in the abstract.

% [PENDING]
  Keywords are terms used in a dissertation for indexing, reflecting core information of the dissertation.
  An abstract may contain a maximum of 5 keywords, with semi-colons used in between to separate one another.

  % Use comma as separator when inputting
  \thusetup{
    keywords* = {keyword 1, keyword 2, keyword 3, keyword 4, keyword 5},
  }
\end{abstract*}
