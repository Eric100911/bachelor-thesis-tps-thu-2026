# 论文结构大纲

## 正文

1. `data/chap01-introduction.tex`
   1.1 Physics Motivation — MPS/MPI intro, J/ψ utility (qualitative, no NRQCD)
   1.2 Experimental Progress in MPS Studies
     1.2.1 Experimental Studies of DPS — cross-channel σ_eff overview
     1.2.2 Double-Quarkonium Measurements — J/ψ-pair, Υ-pair
     1.2.3 Triple-Quarkonium — first access to TPS (Shao–Zhang, CMS)
     1.2.4 Extensions to φ Final States — bridge to J/ψ+J/ψ+φ
   1.3 The J/ψ+J/ψ+φ Channel
   1.4 Thesis Goals and Scope
   1.5 Thesis Organization

   2.1 Proton Structure and PDFs — collinear PDFs, why insufficient for MPS
   2.2 Single and Multiple Parton Scattering — SPS/DPS/TPS definitions, dPDFs
   2.3 Pocket Formulae and Effective Cross Sections — DPS/TPS formulae, σ_eff
   2.4 Heavy-Quarkonium Production in QCD — NRQCD, CSM/COM/CEM, prompt/nonprompt
   2.5 MPS in Multi-Quarkonium Final States — triple-J/ψ, implications for J/ψ+J/ψ+φ
